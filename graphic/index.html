MONSQUAD means a team of monster villains who are passionate about skating. It is a portmanteau of ‘monster’ and ‘squad.’
The brand design focuses on the free-spirited and rebellious nature of teenagers.
These are manifested as a skull with a broken board on its head. The design was made to be approachable by style-minded teenagers.
Additionally, the color mixtures of pink and black represent bright and stable feelings.
These all combine to express the chaotic maturity of teenagers.

This poster was made with a Christmas dessert market theme. I used red and green to convey the feeling of Christmas as well as to make the dessert theme stand out more.
The Christmas colors around the dessert are toned down so that one focuses on the dessert in the middle.
The duck boat and pond symbolize Suseong Lake, a famous area in Daegu. And lastly, the people and food trucks represent the concept of a market.
The hope is that all the elements combine to make the event easily understood at first glance.

The beef stew project is very special to me. As the Product Marketing Manager, I created the idea for a new product, braised beef feet, after conducting market research and discovery.
I handled all the planning, marketing and launch campaigns, selling 20,000 pieces in 6 months and tripling sales at a marketing cost of $500.
Due to the beef stew’s spiciness, the package design uses fire and red to intuitively catch the eye.
The end-to-end processes from the idea to execution are my own work. Only at the very end did we outsource the manufacturing of the packages I designed.
Additionally, in the case of the online product detail page: the current product sales status, problem identification, problem solution, differentiation, quality certificate, review reliability emphasis, cooking method, product specification, and finish were planned in order.
The primary target audience was young women in their 20s and 30s. Therefore I wanted to increase the cleanliness and reliability by using ivory colors throughout. I personally worked on the design, copywriting, image source, etc.
